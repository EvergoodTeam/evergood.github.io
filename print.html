Evergood Reforged

Evergood Reforged reimagines its 1.12.2 Counterpart in the newer versions of Minecraft, starting from 1.18. This means even more Quests and Complex Recipes, all in a new Landscape accompanied by new and returning Mods

As the name implies, this Modpack runs on the Forge ModLoader, meaning that there may be some changes from its Fabric equivalent

Evergood Utilities

Evergood Utilities is a Minecraft Mod for 1.12.2 consisting of a collection of Items, Blocks, Fluids and Recipes, as well as Commands and more created as integration between other Mods and to help Pack Developers.
