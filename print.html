Tech, Magic, a Huge World to Explore, Progression-driven Quests and Complex Recipes, all in 1.12.2

That's Evergood!


The final objective with the Modpack is creating an everlasting and fresh Minecraft Experience, by challenging the Player with Complex Recipes, the Automation of Everything and Adventures in a Beatiful and Unexplored Landscape

Evergood Reforged

Evergood Reforged reimagines its 1.12.2 Counterpart in the newer versions of Minecraft, starting from 1.18. This means even more Quests and Complex Recipes, all in a new Landscape accompanied by new and returning Mods

As the name implies, this Modpack runs on the Forge ModLoader, meaning that there may be some changes from its Fabric equivalent

Evergood Utilities

Evergood Utilities is a Minecraft Mod for 1.12.2 consisting of a collection of Items, Blocks, Fluids and Recipes, as well as Commands and more general Tools created as Integration between other Mods and to help Pack Developers


Currently supported mods are:

  • Tinkers' Construst
  • Thermal Expansion/Foundation
  • Astral Sorcery
  • Mekanism
  • Immersive Engineering
  • Nuclearcraft
  • Biomes O' Plenty
  • Extra Planets

Current additions:

  • Zinc, Palladium, Mercury
    Cinnabar Ore
    Starmetal / Aquamarine Blocks
    Nuggets for Nuclearcraft Ingots, BOP Gems and for Lapis
  • Items used as Icons (useful for a Quest Book)
  • Commands to make some operations faster (making it always daytime, nightvision, spectator)
