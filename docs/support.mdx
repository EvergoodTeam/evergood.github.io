# Supporting the Evergood Projects

There are many different ways you can show support for the Evergood Projects: even just playing with our creations is more than enough! You shouldn't feel any sort of obligation towards us, so feel free to simply enjoy them and have a great time.  

If you do want to show greater support and help continue development, here are listed some options.

### BisectHosting

<a href = 'https://bisecthosting.com/Libra'><Image img={require('@site/static/img/sponsorBanner.png')} /></a><br />

A very direct way to show support for the Projects is making a purchase over at [**BisectHosting**](https://bisecthosting.com/Libra) using our promotional code, **`Libra`**, to get a 25% discount on your order!

### Translations

<a href = 'https://crowdin.com/profile/EvergoodTeam'><Image img={require('@site/static/img/crowdinBanner.png')} /></a><br />

Help translate projects in other languages over at [**Crowdin**](https://crowdin.com/profile/EvergoodTeam)!

### Joining the community

<a href = 'https://discord.gg/k2P68Y8'><Image img={require('@site/static/img/discordBanner.png')} /></a><br />

You can join the community over at our [**Discord Server**](https://discord.gg/k2P68Y8), where you can discuss Projects currently in development, be notified on news about upcoming updates, receive support on any kind of issue you're having or help with development.
